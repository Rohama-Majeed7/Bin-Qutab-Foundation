Nursing Homes

Sub-Urban Areas and Near By Tertiary Care Hospitals in Big Cities.

experienced that the Patients, particularly the Poor families, from the Rural Area when they are brought to Tertiary Care Hospitals in the Cities, If they are not admitted there on the same, they have no place to live in the city. Thereby they return back to those far flanged areas from where they had come from. BIN QUTAB FOUNDATION have planned a solution – BQF would set up Nursing Homes in the Sub-Urban Area as well as Nearby the Tertiary Care Hospital, where they could be taken care for a day or few days till they are admitted to any Tertiary care Hospitals. Along with Patients, normally there two to three persons who come Along with the Patient from the Rural Area, they would also be accommodated and taken care by our Nursing Homes.
