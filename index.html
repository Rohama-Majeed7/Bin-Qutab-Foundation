chairman message

We certainly have come a long way from being a “Cargo Company” in the ‘80’s to currently one of the largest Supply Chain Management & General Trading Group in the region. The long journey has not been without its toil and pain. But we at Bin Qutab regard every obstacle in the path as a challenge to our own capabilities and an experience in a perpetual learning process. Our achievements have been based on the time tested principles of loyalty, dedication and discipline within our ranks and the effective utilization of experience gained and resources developed within our inventory. These are all harnessed by the project management software incorporating planning, monitoring and estimating tools that we have developed on our own in-house! Thank you for visiting us. We hope it has been useful and we look forward to serve you in future.

Muhammad Bashir Malik

Chairman (Founder)
