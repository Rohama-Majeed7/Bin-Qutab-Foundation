volunteers

volunteer programmed

Volunteers are back bone of any civil society organization anywhere in the world. Bin Qutab Foundation is focused on Health Care projects delivery in Rural Pakistan. In the context of Rural the Volunteers becomes so important and helpful that without their contribution the project services delivery becomes very difficult. The basis reason is that The Rural Population is so scattered and when it comes to the Health Care their knowledge about preventive as well as curative medical practices and beliefs are mostly dependent on The Quakes in their village or Goth. We train our Volunteers both Male and Female and they interact with the local communities and work as social mobilizer on behalf of BIN QUTAB FOUNDATION and BEGUM NOOR MEMORIAL HOSITALs network.

Presently we have planned to deploy the Volunteers Team with the following task.

COMMUNITY SUPPORT PROGRAMME
  • Reaching to communities
  • Data Collection
  • Offering solutions to the problems in collaboration with our Donors and Partners.

An APP is being developed which would be available to all and each Volunteer attached with BIN QUTAB FOUNDATION for Collection of data for the following.

  • Who are the sick with the deadly diseases, disability or addiction of any kind as well as persons who needs Palliative Care? They are poor and need BQF help for their treatment. BQF manage their Medical Treatment either bringing them into SEHAT SAHULAT CARD FOLD thru Government Programmed or They would be treated by the Funds from BQF Donors.
  • Who are Special persons / disabled persons who would be enrolled with RUBINA REHABILITATION CENTRE at BEGUM NOOR MEMORIAL HOSPITAl? For their Rehabilitation, Medical or otherwise any surgical proceeded needed by them.
  • Who are the Poor Elderly persons/Widows/Orphans, for whom their families and relatives are unable to support due to their own poverty? ELDERLY PERSONS and WIDOWS – BQF plan to set up a 2 Blocks each for Male and Female to house these Persons and support them for their Decent living as well as Medical Treatment.
  • Who are the skilled persons/Professionals who needs a startup as some SME Business so that they are able to business as well as create more jobs for others?
  • Who are the Natives of these villages who were born and brought here and now have settled in Urban Area in Pakistan and are well settled either somewhere? At Corporate Organization/Public Sector/Groups or managing their personal business SME/Traders/Industrial/Commercial Projects. These Natives would be asked to play their part for the support program of BQF for their fellow ancestor’s village fellows.
  • Who are the Public and Private organization which are working in their village/town for all the above Social welfare services. BQF plan to partner with these already existing organization to synergize the effort for the welfare of the rural masses there.
