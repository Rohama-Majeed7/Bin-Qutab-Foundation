Rural Natives Initiatives

The Rural Natives Initiatives

BQF is generously supported by the Bin Qutub Sons, overseas Pakistanis, natives of Rural Chakwal, BQF Trustees and Donors. This support has enabled us to establish and run the BNM Network of Hospitals in Pakistan. We firmly believe that Pakistan RURAL NATIVES INITIATIVE for love of their own land is making a great difference in meeting the needs of Rural HealthCare and Education Development in Pakistan.

  1. Sardar Muhammad Tanvir Ilyas Khan (Centaurus islamabad) establishing 200 Bed Hospital in his native Village Bangoin Rawla Kot’ AJK.
  2. Governor Punjab Choudhry Ghulam Sarwar long back established a Hospital in his native Tehsil in Punjab.
  3. Prime Minister Imran Khan (besides SKMH) established a world class Nimal University in MianWali his native district.
  4. Brig Fateh Muhammad Malik, CEO Al-Mustafa Trust, a natives of Dunda Shah Bilawal – Talagang/Chakwal had established a Hospital there.
  5. Choudhry Muhammad Ilyas, former Additional Auditor General of Pakistan, a native of Kala Gujran/Jehlum in collaboration with BIN QUTAB FOUNDATION And his Family is establishing a Hosopital there.
  7. Muneere Hussain Bukhari, former Chief Financial Officer of SHIFA HOSPITAL INTERNAITOAL, Islamabad, a native of Pind Dadan Khan, Islamabad in collaboration with BIN QUTAB FOUNDATION and PIND DADAN KHAN Welfare Society and Mr.Yasin Baloch a BusinessMan/Philanthropis are establishing a Medial and Kidney Dialysis centre at PIND DADAN KHAN.
  8. Mr.Muhmmad Sakhi an overseas Pakistani in USA establishing a World Class .. Mega Sports Villlage in Kamoon Kee with Billions of Rupees budget
  9. A National Singer Mr. Ibrar ul Haq now Chairman Pakistan Red Crescent established a Big Hospital in his native area of Narowal.

There are numerous other examples of these Philanthropists who have done great contributions in their Natives Districts where humanity is benefiting day and night. There is an urgent need of a Central coordination team with a Strategic Plan with participation of Federal’ Provincial Governments” Corporate Sector CSR ‘ Media’ ‘and Philanthropic participation of other National Donors’ Join hands together for Many Big Projects which can cover almost 150 plus Districts in Rural Pakistan needs as well as City Slums which are extensions of rural migration.

BQF Call

BQF requests every person blessed with success in his life to take care of his native rural hometown and contribute to its development. BQF’s successful healthcare model can be replicated and managed by BQF professional team. The project can be supported by other partners and donors of that rural area.

The Salient Feature of BQF Healthcare Program

  • The customizable healthcare program as per the needs of rural area
  • The focus on improving health and quality of life
  • Sustainable program through multi-partner support
  • Sponsored by local natives and managed by the BQF team
  • Highly professional, certified, and standardized healthcare practices
  • Involvement of local natives and well-wishers
  • Connected with main BNMH hospital online
  • Healthcare is followed by a complete rural development program

The Great Action

You as the native of some rural area, please own your birth-place. We will provide you the entire plan from a humble to start of healthcare setup to a complete rural development plan.
